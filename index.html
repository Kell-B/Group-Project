Since 1985, Alveolus has been committed to cleaning the air you breathe!

No matter what the air is like outside, you can ensure that the air you breathe inside your home or office is safe and clean. Our passion for protecting clean air for every individual has led us to develop state-of-the-art air filters that can have been scientifically proven to reduce air pollution particulates by 99%. In order to make clean air affordable for everybody, we have dedicated decades of research to developing more effective air filtration technology with more efficient production methods.

The clean air revolution has arrived!

We attack air pollution from the outside-in. Filtering your home or office is just the final step in providing you with clean air. Safeguarding the environment is the first step. In all of our production facilities, we have employed the most cutting-edge technologies to reduce air, water, and soil pollution from our manufacturing processes. Additionally, all our filters are made from biodegradable material that is sustainably sourced. By buying our filters, you aren't just cleaning your own air, you're cleaning everybody's air!
